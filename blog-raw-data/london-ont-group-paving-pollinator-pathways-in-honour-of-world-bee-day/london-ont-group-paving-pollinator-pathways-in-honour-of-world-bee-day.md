We were featured in 2020 by Global News for World Bee Day. Here's an excerpt, check out the full article here: [https://globalnews.ca/news/6978736/london-ontario-world-bee-day/](https://globalnews.ca/news/6978736/london-ontario-world-bee-day/)

"In honour of [World Bee Day](https://globalnews.ca/tag/world-bee-day/) on Wednesday, a local community-led organization is buzzing about the idea of sprucing up the city’s bee-friendly gardens.

Alex Leonard with the London, Ont., [Pollinator Pathways Project](https://www.pollinatorpathwaysproject.com/), a volunteer group that works to educate people about pollinator pathways within the city, spoke with 980 CFPL’s Jess Brady on The Afternoon Show Thursday about the group’s new projects.
