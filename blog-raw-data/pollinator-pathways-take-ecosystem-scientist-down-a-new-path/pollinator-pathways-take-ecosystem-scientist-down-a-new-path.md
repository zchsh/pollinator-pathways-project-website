"I have immersed myself in my community in London, Ontario and together with some neighbours and friends we have posed the question: Can we turn, or rather, how do we turn an entire city into a pollinator sanctuary?" said Dr. Gabor Sass. Check out the full article here by Science Rendezvous: [https://www.sciencerendezvous.ca/innovation_showcase/2020/08/12/pollinator-pathways-take-ecosystem-scientist-down-a-new-path/](https://www.sciencerendezvous.ca/innovation_showcase/2020/08/12/pollinator-pathways-take-ecosystem-scientist-down-a-new-path/) We have so much potential in Canadian cities as millions of hectares of urban land is dedicated to pollinator unfriendly lawns. What if we converted a portion of that acreage to pollinator friendly yards and meadows?
